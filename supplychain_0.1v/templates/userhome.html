Agriculture
Product SupplyChain

Our site is used for exchange of agricultural products between the manufacturer and the farmers through a delivery partners. The remarkable blockchain technology used provides high security in every aspect.





Previous Next


The Manufacturer:

The producer of goods.

All the raw materials required for production are gathered by the manufacturing company. The company starts production of the end product. Then,the product is fixed for price. Finally, they are displayed on the site and ready to be sold. Also, the manufacturer accepts orders from clients, packs the order and dispatches the package to the delivery company.

The Logistics Company:

Delivering the package safely.

The logistic company is tied up with the manufacturing company in delivering the package to the farmers. The package is transferred from one agent to the other in the process of delivery. Finally, the order is delivered to the farmer on verifying it with an OTP. All these details can be tracked by the farmer as a user.

The farmer:

The end user.

The requestor of goods from the site is the farmer. The ultimate aim of the project is to fulfill the needs of farmer through a Secure System. As an end user the farmer has access to his order all the way from the manufacturer company to himself. This process does not involve any intervention by unauthorized people.

About Us

We are team of four looking to develop an application for the use of farmers situated in remote locations of the country. This project is intended to supply agricultural products to farmers directly from the manufacturer. We have developed this application as a part of final year project under the guidance of our lecturer Ms.Babitha M N. We strive to give our best in this project to help farmers.
